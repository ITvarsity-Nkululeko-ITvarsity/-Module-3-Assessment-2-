Banana Milkshake

Prep time: 5 mins

Yield: 01

Serving size: 1 Glass (600 ml)

Price: R55

Ingredients





Steps

Step 1

Making this banana milkshake is incredibly easy! All you need to do is add the ice cream , milk, and frozen banana to a blender.

Step 2

Then just blend until smooth! Give it a taste and add some maple syrup if you want your milkshake to be a bit sweeter.

Step 3

If you can't get your milkshake blended, add a bit more milk very slowly. If you add too much your milkshake will become too thin.
